Who We Are

Robopanda Robotic & Coding is a learning platform focused on introducing robotics and coding through logic, creativity, and play.

We believe that understanding systems should start simple — through exploration, experimentation, and meaningful projects.

What We Teach

Logical Thinking

Problem-solving through games, puzzles, and structured challenges.

Coding Fundamentals

From Scratch-based projects to basic programming concepts.

Robotics Concepts

Understanding how software interacts with real-world systems.

Our Direction

This platform is designed to grow step by step. Starting from basic learning materials, it will gradually evolve into a structured space for robotics and coding education.
